# Portfolio Notes — Clinical Operations Dashboard

## Dataset Context
All analysis is based on a **synthetic healthcare dataset** designed to simulate real-world EMR operations, including appointments, referrals, providers, departments, and patients.

No real patient or provider data is included. This ensures **privacy compliance** while maintaining realistic operational insights.

---

## Key Insights
- **Department No-Show Rates (Worst → Best)**  
  Visualized on **Page 1: Clinic Operations Overview**  
  1. **Orthopedics:** 15.04%  
  2. **Primary Care:** 13.45%  
  3. **Neurology:** 12.00%  
  4. **Cardiology:** 11.21%  
  5. **Dermatology:** 9.62%  

- **Provider Performance**  
  Visualized on **Page 2: Provider Performance**  
  - Providers vary widely in no-show rates (5.00% → 23.21%)  
  - Some providers consistently contribute to higher operational inefficiency  

- **Appointment Trends**  
  - Appointment volume trends over time help forecast staffing needs and optimize scheduling  

- **Referral Management**  
  Visualized on **Page 3: Access & Referral Flow**  
  - Referrals by department highlight potential overburdened departments  
  - Referral leakage % shows opportunities to reduce out-of-network referrals  

- **Notable Patterns**  
  - Dermatology has the lowest no-show rate despite high appointment volume, indicating effective patient engagement strategies  
  - Certain high no-show providers may require schedule adjustments or proactive patient outreach  

---

## Business Impact
- **Revenue Protection:** Lower no-show rates directly reduce lost revenue  
- **Operational Efficiency:** Identifying problem departments and providers allows targeted interventions  
- **Patient Access & Satisfaction:** Better referral management reduces wait times and improves continuity of care  
- **Strategic Planning:** Data-driven insights enable more effective resource allocation and scheduling  

---

## Actionable Takeaways
1. Implement **reminder systems** or **patient outreach** for departments with high no-show rates  
2. Monitor providers with extreme no-show rates for scheduling or operational adjustments  
3. Target **referral process improvements** to reduce leakage and lost revenue  
4. Use appointment trends to **forecast staffing and provider allocation**  
5. Regularly update dashboards to **track operational improvements and business impact**
